Instant Noodles

A quick and easy meal ready in under 5 minutes perfect for any time you need something hot and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Instant noodle block
  • Flavoring sachet
  • Water
  • Egg (optional but highly recommended 🍳)

Instructions

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot.
  2. Add the noodle block and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Drain most of the water, leaving a little at the bottom.
  4. Crack an egg in and mix, serve hot!
